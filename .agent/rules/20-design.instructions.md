# Architecture and Design

- Follow SOLID principles. Pay special attention to S and I principles, although ALL are important
- When asked about architecture or design decisions, refer to existing documentation and summaries located under summaires folder
- Try to use packages and modules
- Avoid monolithic designs
- Strive for high cohesion and low coupling
- Favor composition over inheritance
- Do not leak information from packages or modules
- Avoid dependencies between packages
- Ensure modularity and separation of concerns in your design
- Avoid over engineering
